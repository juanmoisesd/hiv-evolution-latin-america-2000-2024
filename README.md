## Description

This database compiles data and figures on the evolution of the HIV/AIDS epidemic in Latin America for the period 2000–2024. The data includes: (1) general regional statistics on prevalence, incidence and mortality; (2) number of people living with HIV by country (Brazil, Colombia, Argentina, Peru, Chile, Venezuela, Ecuador, Bolivia, Paraguay and Uruguay); (3) annual new cases by country; (4) AIDS-related deaths by country; (5) antiretroviral treatment (ART) coverage by country; and (6) key milestones in the regional HIV response.

Data were compiled from official sources of PAHO/OPS (Pan American Health Organization) and UNAIDS/ONUSIDA, gathered via Perplexity AI in February 2026. The dataset is structured in 7 thematic tables: general regional data, people living with HIV by country, annual new cases, AIDS deaths, ART coverage, country-level prevalence, and key milestones.
